About Us

We are a small family business, run by myself, Eleni, my husband Kosta and our children Christos and Maria.

In 2017 we opened our Mini Market in Vitalades with the aim of being an alternative to the distant supermarkets, both for the villagers and for the numerous tourists who visit our village.

In order to achieve this goal, we have set up a wide range of products in our shop, so you can enjoy your vacation and do not have to go far.

We are working hard to offer you something very special. Your wishes are at the top of our priority list.
